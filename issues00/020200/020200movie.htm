Movie Reviews: Scream 3 & Gone in 60 Seconds
Brian Hale / Entertainment Editor

Scream 3

After graduation, walking killer-magnet Neve Campbell heads to Hollywood to pursue an acting career. 

Unfortunately, she lands her first role in a B-type horror film which takes her full circle back to Woodsboro, the scene of the original crime. 

Life then takes on the eerie feel of déjà vu as the actors in the production get whacked off one at a time. 

Scream 3 stars David Arquette, Neve Campbell, Courteney Cox, Parker Posey, and Patrick Dempsey. The film is once again directed by Wes Craven.


Gone In 60 Seconds

A car thief, played by Nicolas Cage, agrees to lead his gang one last time before they retire. They decide to take on the challenge of stealing fifty cars in a single night to save his brother’s life (Ribisi) from the mob. 

The problem (besides the obvious logistical obstacles) is that another gang is aiming for the same heist, and the police (including Olyphant) are after them too. 

This film stars actors Nicolas Cage, Giovanni Ribisi, Scott Caan, James Duval, Robert Duvall, and Angelina Jolie.
