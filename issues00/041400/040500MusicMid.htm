Music Midtown 2000:
Atlanta’s Biggest Music Festival

Bored with things going on around Columbus? Do you love concerts? Well, if you are looking for something exciting and adventurous to do in the near future, then make plans now to attend one of the biggest music festivals known to man, Atlanta’s own Music Midtown! This year promises to be one of the biggest and best ever, with 9 outdoor stages and lots of performers! Here’s all the info you need in order to make plans to attend!

When: May 5-7, 2000

Where: Atlanta- from Ralph McGill to Pine Street and Piedmont to Bedford Place. That includes Renaissance Park and Bedford Pines Park. Gates are close to the Civic Center and North Avenue MARTA stations.
