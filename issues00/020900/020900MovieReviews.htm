Brian Hale / Entertainment Editor

Holy Smoke 

Starring: Kate Winslet , Harvey Keitel , Tim Rogers , Sophie Lee and Pam Grier

Ruth, a beautiful young Australian, went to India in search of answers and found herself quickly seduced by a mysterious world completely different from her own. 

When news reached her family, they imagined the worst, so they hired a top American cult buster to bring her back. 
This young seeker is turning the tables on this spiritual pro and leading him into an all-out test of wills and game of temptation.



Simpatico

Starring: Nick Nolte , Jeff Bridges , Sharon Stone , Loyd Catlett and Shawn Hatosy

Set in the fast-paced world of thoroughbred racing, Simpatico is the story of a high-stakes deception and the ultimate price paid for a friendship bound by betrayal. Having made a career out of playing the odds, best friends Lyle Carter (Bridges) and Vinnie Webb (Nolte) and Vinnie’s then-girlfriend Rosie (Stone), hatch a plan to execute the ultimate score. 

The plan goes awry when Lyle and Rosie run off with their take. Partners in crime, the three find themselves bound by a secret and yet divided by a betrayal of the heart. Twenty years later, Vinnie is back to settle the score and not even he realizes that they will all pay for their deception.
