Streaking: it's good for you!

by Brian Hale / Entertainment Editor
I rarely ever get behind any kind of campaign or drive with any kind of force or intent. That is just not the type of thing that does it for me.

In the midst of taking a Political Communication class, right here at local election time, one would think that getting excited about a campaign would be easy. Nope. It’s not, until now! Here is something we can all get behind and show some support for: STREAKING!

Hear me out. It has recently been brought to my attention that streaking has been a problem on campus in the past. For those not following me, yes, people have run across OUR campus bare bottomed naked. My question is, why is this considered a problem?

With all of this 40th Anniversary talk going on, I think it would be appropriate to bring back some of the practices of old, and streaking is as good as any.

All we are lacking is a few volunteers. This is where you guys come in. You’ve seen the signs around campus that say, “The Saber needs your help blah blah.” Well, we do. This can’t be done without you. Nobody, and I mean nobody, wants to see any of us from the Saber streak naked anywhere, much less in public. So, it’s up to you. If there are any volunteers, drop me a line at Loxley32@aol.com, or just drop by the Saber office. Thanks.
