Falcons improve
by Chris Anderson / Sports Editor

HOME

Although the Atlanta Falcons did not defeat the San Francisco 49ers last weekend they did show potential.
The Falcons of old would have given up after a 31-7 half time deficit. However, they hung tough and added two touchdowns in the forth quarter to make the score 31-20.

The Falcons’ main problem, however, is that they continue to make too many mistakes. Jerry Rice had an easy touchdown to open the scoring and Chris Chandler threw for three interceptions which did not help matters.

They hope to improve this weekend as they try to go 2-0 against the Carolina Panthers in the Georgia Dome.
