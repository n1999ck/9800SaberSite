Campus Candid

by Michael Cox / Editor-in-chief
Tabitha Presley is a sophomore at Columbus State University majoring in Communications. She came to CSU from LaGrange, Ga. where she attended LaGrange High School and later graduated in 1997.

Presley is involved in several things on campus. Her activities are social as well as academic. She is an active member of Delta Zeta Sorority where she is the Assistant Rush Chair. Presley is also a tutor for the Prep Tutor Program where she visits various high schools in the area and assists young children in their efforts toward school assignments and other activities.

Another group Presley gives of her time to is P.A.L. for college success class where she serves as a mentor to incoming freshmen at Columbus State University.

Tabitha explains that she hopes to become a family therapist upon graduation. She says, “Coming from a small town like LaGrange, you learn a lot about people.” She continues by stating, “College is a big step for anyone and also a time for a person to mature and get prepared for what lies ahead in the real world.”
