CSU wins Peach State Intercollegiate

by Chris Anderson / Sports Editor
The C.S.U. golf team held up to its #1 ranking in Division II by defeating thirteen other teams to capture their second victory at the the Cherokee Run golf course in September 22.

The team shot a +2 (866), and defeated their nearest competitor Georgia State by four strokes. Leading the way for the Cougars was Jaco Rall who won individual medalist with a -6 (210), followed by Jon Drewery who finished with a -1 (215), and Chad Andre with a +6 (222).

The team hopes to continues its roll as they compete in the Hillman Robbins Memorial in Memphis, October 5-6.
