De Niro - it doesn't get much better

by Brian Hale / Entertainment Editor
It would be extremely difficult for me to choose my favorite actor. The list is full of great performers, such as Andy Garcia, Al Pacino, and Jack Nicholson. However, one man seems to stand out from all the others - Robert De Niro. He is truly what a great performer should be, so I just want to share a bit of information about him with everyone.

De Niro was born on August 17, 1943 in New York, New York. At one time several years ago, he dated model Naomi Campbell - way to go Robert! But, two wives later, he appears to be very happy with Grace Hightower. They were married on June 17, 1997.

The grand total, all relationships combined, gives him three sons and one daughter. They are Drena De Niro, Raphael De Niro, Aaron Kendrick, and Julian Henry.

Here’s some stuff you probably didn’t know, and probably don’t need to know, but I’m telling you anyway.

In his 1980 Oscar acceptance speech he thanked Joey LaMotta, who was at the time suing United Artists Productions over the movie “Raging Bull,” which is a portrayal of LaMotta.

De Niro was the spokesperson for the television commercials for AMC Ambassador automobiles in 1969 and formed his production company, TriBeCa Productions, in 1989.

In the past years, De Niro has graced the cover of many magazine publications, such as “Esquire,” “Premiere,” “Empire,” and “Studio.”

As far as I’m concerned, Robert De Niro is one of the major reasons that the movie industry and “Hollywood” is alive and well today. Don’t believe me? Ronin was originally set for release on October 2, but after several low and disappointing screening scores, they went back into the studio and made a few changes. Scores improved a great deal and the release date is now set for September 25.

In this movie, De Niro plays one of several CIA-like agents who team up for private cases. Things go wrong on a dangerous job in Europe. When the team is double-crossed, each member begins to reveal his true self.

Like I said before, Robert De Niro is one of the greatest performers we will ever see. Not all of the movies listed above are good movies, but I can guarantee you that whatever is wrong with the movie, it sure ain’t De Niro.
