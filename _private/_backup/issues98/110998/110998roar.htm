Last Wednesday in class my professor told about five of us that we were chronic tardy people. Now take it that three of the five came in right at the sound of the 9:00 a.m. clock tower bell and the other and I came in three minutes after that bell. We were only late to about two other of his classes. Threats such as lowering the final project by one letter grade is what we have to contend with if we’re in violation of his self made Constitution. I can understand being late here and there but to out right label students as chronic tardy people is an insult to us students who pay to attend CSU.

Sometimes things come up and you have to arrive late to class. Most professors here at CSU understand the minor problems that come up at the last minute causing students to arrive a few minutes late to class. However, you can clearly see that’s not the case with this professor who is of rare commodity.

I would like to thank all the professors who understand that many of us do have personal lives that may sometime interfere with our class attendance- Your patience and understanding of the legitimate excuses students present is highly appreciated.
