Greek Week a success
by Helen Godwin / Associate Editor

HOME

Campus sororities and fraternities kicked off Greek Week ‘98, spanning from October 25 to October 31 with the theme “Something’s Brewin’.”

The activities for the week included pumpkin painting, karaoke, toga volleyball, goofy games, cookouts, a treasure hunt, and a reverse beauty pageant. The week ended with a Halloween party at the alumni house.

There were awards given for participation and excellence in many events. The honor of being Greek God and Greek Goddess were awarded to Kevin Breemes of Kappa Sigma and Amanda Branson of Delta Zeta. The Banner competition was won by Delta Zeta and Xi Theta was given the Participation award. Able Walker, of Kappa Sigma, won the Reverse Beauty Pageant.

Stephanie Adams, a member of Delta Zeta, said that Greek Week was “an opportunity for all Greek organizations to come together once a year and show support for one another and try to get the message out to non-Greeks as to what we are all about.”
