Students have field day on campus

by Athena Curry / Editor-in-Chief
CSU participated in a recreational field day with our Partner in Education, “30th Avenue Elementary School Thursday, April 2 at the CSU Mock Pavilion.

The purpose of the event was to provide for interaction between the 30th Avenue children and CSU faculty and staff, and treat the students to an “expanded playground”.

Approximately 200 elementary school students and several CSU faculty members and students attended the event.

The day was filled with various activities, including an Easter egg hunt, sack races, marshmallow drop, egg toss, water balloon volleyball and other games.

One CSU student who attended the event says, “This was a really great opportunity for students to get out of the classroom. They enjoyed a day o f fun and furthered their interest in academics.”

The event was organized by Ric Cravens, Campus Recreation Director at CSU and the CSU Staff Council.
