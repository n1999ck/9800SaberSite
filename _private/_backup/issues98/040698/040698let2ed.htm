Letter to the Editor
This letter is in response to your “movie critic” Josh Eller’s review of “The Wedding Singer.” It is important to note that as a movie review columnist, Mr. Eller should not give away the entire plot and storyline. Ruining the entire plot and storyline. Ruining the story for those who have not viewed the film is not a positive requisite of a good journalist. In the future, please offer an overall review without telling the entire story. Nothing is worse than knowing how the story ends before you even get to the theater!
