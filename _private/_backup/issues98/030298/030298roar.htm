What the hell is the problem with our professors? I have a friend that has had many family and financial problems this quarter yet he has still made every effort to attend class as much as possible and to keep up with the work. This friend also recently had another friend that committed suicide in his home town. As you can see this person could use a break. So he tried to continue his classes only to have his professors refuse to work with him and even ask him to withdraw. Why should he have to if he has a chance to pass? I know in some cases the student may make excuses for laziness but not all students do. The professor could take the time to talk to and research the student. I think professors need to remember that students are human and sometimes bad events happen out of their control. We should still have a chance to catch our breath and pass our classes. Under certain circumstances they shouldn’t make it any harder than it already is.
