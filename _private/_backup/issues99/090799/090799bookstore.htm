ROAR!

Letters to the Editor

 

Swarming CSU students at the “used” piles of textbooks while they still lasted, stacks of paper supplies, and workmen installing bookshelves are some of the things you probably saw in the CSU bookstore on the first day of classes. You may have also noticed the new carpet, new fixtures and a wider variety of merchandise.

This past summer F.H.E.G. (Follett Higher Education Group – the independent owners of the bookstore) and CSU made a joint effort to improve the University’s bookstore. Many students may not be aware of the fact that the current bookstore location was closed down and relocated to Davidson, room 256 for about a week while workers completely replaced all carpet and fixtures in the book store.

Store manager, Terry Craig, says that the changes mean more shelf space for a greater variety of merchandise for bookstore patrons. New merchandise includes more computer software, CD’s, clothing, magazines, best seller books and computer accessories such as keyboards and zip drives. The CSU bookstore can also special order items with CSU organization logos and sayings on them.

Craig also says that despite the additions to the bookstore, the textbooks are in their original locations. So, those that are familiar with the layout of the store can still zip in and out with the same ease. Craig is now waiting on easier–to–read signs that will help customers find what they need faster. 

Despite efforts to make shopping easier, there are still some that abhor the thought of shopping. For these people F.H.E.G. has a web site for on-line ordering. Now you can just click on the “bookstore” option on the CSU web sight or go to <http://www.efollett.com> and order the textbooks you need. Books can be ordered for home delivery, reserved for purchase and pickup later or purchased on-line and picked up at the store.

Terry Craig says that she hopes that the recent renovations and advancement will allow the CSU bookstore to continue to grow and change to meet the needs of CSU students and faculty.
