Students needed for Winterfest planning
Candice Walker / Staff Writer

It is considered one of the biggest events at Columbus State. It is a time when students gather together as a family to promote the school to the community, unite the student body, raise school spirit and reconnect with the alumni. It is directed toward the student body, and for the first time, the students will be organizing this grand event. What is it that I am referring to? I am talking about the third annual Winterfest!

In the past, the staff and administration have organized Winterfest activities. According to students, they have not been too impressed with what they have done. Students are looking to make Winterfest a part of Columbus State tradition, and they want you to get involved. Your input and participation will help to make this a successful week for you and your friends. 

To be considered for the Winterfest committee, students should pick up applications in Davidson Center room 271. The applications will be available from August 30 until September 18. Interviews of prospective members will begin on September 20 and continue through October 1. The Winterfest activities will take place the week of February 7-12, 2000. 

If you’re interested in this committee, they are interested in you. Winterfest officials want dedicated people who don’t mind pitching in to help the students have a successful week.

During the week of Winterfest, you will learn about the different organizations that make up CSU and watch as they compete for the title of best organization on campus. The winner will be determined by a point system. Winners will receive a trophy and will have their names placed on a plaque that will hang on campus in a highly visible place to recognize each year’s winning organization. Other prizes will also be issued during the week.

If you want to know what goes on behind the scenes or you just want to come out and have a good time, this is a great opportunity to meet new people and to make new friends. 

For more information please call ext. 2273 or 3586.
