Sixth Annual Music Midtown
Scott Meeks / Guest Writer

For some time now the Atlanta area has been known for it’s great music scene. Many, many musicians have gotten their start by playing, and eventually signing throughout Atlanta and surrounding areas. If you’re not convinced that Atlanta offers musicians and music fans some great opportunities, then next month will be the chance for the city of Atlanta to prove it to you.

At the end of April (April 30th-May 2nd), Atlanta will hold it’s Sixth Annual Music Midtown Festival. Nationally known bands such as: Hole, Goo Goo Dolls, Everclear, Reel Big Fish, Iggy Pop, Big Bad Voodoo Daddy, Widespread Panic, New Radicals, and even Willie Nelson will rock twelve city blocks of “Midtown” Atlanta. The huge festival will also hold a “locals only stage” carrying some of Atlanta and Athens favorite bands. The confirmed bands are obviously a must see, and even more bands are still in the process of signing.

The best news of all is that tickets are only thirty dollars for a three day pass (less- expensive single day tickets are also available). The three-day pass will include over twenty-five hours of great live music for your listening pleasure. TicketMaster, located at your local Publix store have the tickets on sale now. For extra information please contact Music Midtown at musicmidtown.com OR call at 770-MIDTOWN.
