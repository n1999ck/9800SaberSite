Threat of war prompts opinion on campus
Clarissa Andrews / Staff Writer

By this point in time, almost everyone knows something about what is going on in Yugoslavia, even if it’s just that NATO forces initiated a large air strike last week.

Even students who rarely watch the news or read newspapers know a few details about the situation. While students might not have cared about what was happening on the other side of the world a month ago, the possibility of going to war has stirred up some opinions.  MORE

Remera will be joined by African-activist colleagues Bakary Tandia (human rights/contemporary slavery) and signer/songwriter Rebecca Wakuteka. MORE
