All Greek mixer huge success at CSU
The Brother of Kappa Sigma

Contrary to popular belief, the Greeks at CSU can coexist. This past Monday night, Kappa Sigma hosted an All Greek Mixer at Rae’s Pub. The Greek bash was a huge success and all organizations present had great representation. Among the participants were Sigma Nu, Phi Mu Kappa, Tau Kappa Epsilon, Delta Zeta, Kappa Sigma, Xi Theta, and Sigma Omega.

While Greek Week and Winterfest provide an opportunity for Greeks to get involved, these events can often create strong competition between organizations.

At this event, there were no best participation plaques or awards for enthusiasm. Monday night was strictly a chance for all fraternities and sororities to just socialize and get to know each other better, without all the competition.

Monday night also provided a great opportunity for rush. Some students present were not Greek, but have been interested in rushing. Hopefully, the unity displayed this past week will persuade these students to find out more about the Greek way of life here at CSU.

Because of the huge turn out of both Greek and non-Greek students, more of these mixers will be scheduled in the near future and, hopefully, the Greek system here at CSU will grow even more than it has in recent years.

Kappa Sigma appreciates everyone’s participation and support and hopes that this is the only the beginning of a new brand of Greek unity here on campus.
