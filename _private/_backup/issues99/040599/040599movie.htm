Movie Buzz
Brian Hale / Entertainment Editor

“The Matrix”

In the near future, a computer hacker named Neo, played by Keanu Reeves, discovers that all life on Earth is nothing more than an elaborate smoke-screen created by a intelligent cyber-goon, for the purpose of keeping us busy while our life essence is “farmed” to fuel the Matrix’s campaign of domination in the “real” world. Neo joins rebel warriors Morpheus, played by Laurence Fishburne and Trinity, played by Carrie Ann Moss in their struggle to overthrow the Matrix.

“EDtv”

EDtv follows the strange, hilarious and sometimes painful ordeals suffered by the members of a family when the youngest son, Ed, a slacker video store clerk, is taken from his everyday life and becomes the blockbuster hit of the season when he agrees to have his “life” aired on cable TV 24 hours-a-day. I thought this was a great movie - I laughed almost the whole movie. Matthew McConaughey put together a wonderful performance. McConaughey is joined by Jenna Elfman, Woody Harrelson, Ellen DeGeneres, and Dennis Hopper. The film is directed by Ron Howard.

“Forces Of Nature”

Ben has to get from New York to Savannah in one day for his wedding to Bridget. He has everything under control until a startling woman named Sarah literally falls into his life... and everything he thought he had planned seems to fall apart rather nicely. One thing leads to another, there is a plane crash, a train derailment and an arrest, but Ben is falling for Sarah despite his pending wedding and all the other distractions. And, oh yea, the feeling is decidedly mutual. So what does Ben do? Will it be the new and exciting lady or the love of his life in Savannah? “Forces Of Nature” stars Sandra Bullock, Ben Affleck, Maura Tierney, Steve Zahn, and Ronny Cox.
