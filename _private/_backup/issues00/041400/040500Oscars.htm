This year’s Oscar goes to... 
Jenny Kump / Entertainment Writer

The golden man has struck again. Like Santa Claus and the Easter Bunny. He comes at night and bestows gifts upon special people. 
Normally to be included in the group of special people you have to have been in one of the best movies of the year, have given a spectacular preformance in that movie, and gone far above your abilities in show business. 

But, not this year. All you had to do to get an Oscar this year was dress up like a boy, get involved in a time warp life, or have fantasies about your daughters friend. 

That’s who they gave awards to this year. I must say that I am sorely disappointed in the Oscars. 

Why do they give them out to the most undeserving people? I just don’t get it. 

It must be really easy to get one because I didn’t see very much talent among those winners. 

Take Hilary Swank for example. She won an Oscar because she dressed up like a boy and gave a marvelous performance. 
NO. She won an Oscar because she was in a contravercial movie. 
She could have given the worst performance of her life and she still would have won because of the type of movie she did. 

I bet George Lucas is upset. After all the work he put into the sure fire winner of Pahntom Menace, the Matrix stole all his awards. 

Yeah, it deserved the award for best special effects but to win the one for sound and those others as well? 

It wasn’t that great. When they showed Keanu on screen last night, all he said was “Woah!” 

Now my personal unfavorite of the night—American Beauty. 

I hated it. I still cannot understand why it was nominated for anything. It was a sick and twisted movie. 

I am sure fans of the movie think that I just missed the point when I watched it, but I promise I didn’t. 

I understand all the mumbo jumbo of there being “too much beauty in the world, even in death,” but I cannot handle the fact that it won so much. 

Kevin Sapcey as best actor? Please!! I still believe that half of those ballots are missing...the intelligent half that didn’t vote for that movie. 
How about the 11 year old Haley Joel Osment getting shafted? Out of all the people in that room that kid deserved an Oscar most of all. Now there’s an actor. 

I did like Michael Caine’s acceptance speech. It was very noble and humble of him, and that impressed me. 

However, they had to give him the Oscar you know. I guess they figured that they have a lot more years left out of the kid and Michael’s getting a bit too old. 

I was happy with some of the winners. Applause for Angelina Jolie for winning Best Supporting Actress for her performance in Girl Interrupted. 

She was fantastic. However, last night she looked like she walked out of the “Night of the Living Dead.” 

Normally she looks so pretty. And what’s up with her obsession with her brother??? Hmmmm. Not even gonna touch that one. 

Congratulations should also go out to Phil Collins. Tarzan was a great movie and he made it that way with the music. He deserved it. 

As for the rest of the night...yawn. Oh, and what happened to Fight Club? 

Now there’s a movie that deserves to be nominated for something. The Oscars used to be a prestegious thing, but I’m not sure what they are now. 

Next year, when the little gold man comes to make his rounds, I hope he checks his list twice because it looks to me like he forgot it this year.

He forgot who was good. He forgot eveything! 

Good luck next year. If your planning on being in a movie next year be as horrible as you can be. You might actually win an Oscar.
