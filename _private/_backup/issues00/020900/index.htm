Astronaut Guy Bluford visits Columbus State
 Jessica Trenchik / Features Editor

Columbus State University was recently honored with the presence of Dr. Guy Bluford, the first African American to go into space, here on campus. Bluford, CSU’s Black History Month keynote speaker, was on campus February 3 to give a talk about his career as an astronaut, life in space, and the future of space exploration.

Jasper Cooke, Director of Public Safety, moving on
Chad Wayne / News Editor

Jasper Cooke, Director of Public Safety at CSU has accepted a position at Augusta State University. He leaves CSU on Feb. 25, 2000, to fulfill his employment at ASU in a position similar to his here at CSU.

$10,000 in tools stolen from gym work site; brothers arrested
Chad Wayne / News Editor

On Sunday, Dec. 5, 1999, at approximately 3:30 in the afternoon, two brothers stole over $10,000 worth of tools from the work site of the new gym. According to Jasper Cooke, Director of Public Safety, the two thieves have been arrested and are awaiting trial by the state court.
