And there’s more!! To get the complete list of dates and times for shows and other bands, check out www.ticketmaster.com. You can search by name of band, state, or even venue if you know where you’d like to go. 

If you or someone you know is interested in purchasing tickets for any of these great concerts, make sure you get your tickets early. The sooner the better, because the best seats always sell out first. 
There are also a few good tips to remember if you plan to attend any summer concerts. Here’s a list of things to know:

If the concert is outside, like at Lakewood Amphitheater or Atlanta Motor Speedway, 

1. Take LOTS of sunscreen! Nothing is worse than a bad sunburn.

2. Remember to drink lots of water. Dehydration can make you very sick.

3. If there are no rules against bringing your own snacks, do so! Food at concerts can be VERY expensive! 

4. Don’t consume too much alcohol. Dehydration can occur faster if you are drinking too much alcohol and sweating a lot at the same time.

5. Don’t forget to take some blankets to sit on! Sitting in dirt is not much fun!

6. Always have a raincoat. You never know when a storm could pop up.

7. Take a change of clothes so that you won’t be smelly on the way home. 

If the concert is indoors like at Philips Arena, Take a jacket!! It can be chilly in there.

Take snacks if you are allowed. Food is pricey! You might spend more on food than you did on the tickets.

Dress appropriately. You don’t want to look like a beach bum for a nice event. Allow time to find a parking place. Parking can be far away from the event, especially in Atlanta.

Don’t take a huge bag inside. It might not be allowed, and if it is you don’t want to keep up with a lot of stuff in a cramped area.

If you are allergic to cigarette smoke, stay away from small venues. Many small theaters and clubs do not discourage smoking, and you might suffocate. 

These are just a few tips for concert fun. Make sure you don’t miss any good shows! There are some once in a lifetime concerts being offered this summer and you don’t want to miss out! I know I won’t! See you there!!
