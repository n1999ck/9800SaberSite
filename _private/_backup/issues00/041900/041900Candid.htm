Campus Candid: Angie Pilkington
Jessica Trenchik / Features Editor

This week’s Campus Candid is the sweet and philanthropic Angie Pilkington. Angie is a junior this year at CSU.

When asked about her major, Angie replies, “It’s currently early childhood education but as soon as I get to the office to change it it’s going to be recreation.”

Angie comes to Columbus from Ringgold, GA. Ringgold is about five minutes from Chattanooga, TN where Angie says she “does all [her] business.”

“Honestly, the housing and my friend Jamie,” Angie replies when asked why she chose to come to CSU.

Angie has been working as a camp counselor during the summer for six years now.

“I worked at a private Christian camp for three years called Camp Joy,” says Angie. “And then I worked for three years at Camp Adahi, which is on top of Lookout Mountain. It’s a Girl Scout camp. I served two years as a camp counselor and one year as a unit leader.”
“I hope to own and run my own summer camp for underprivileged children,” says Angie of her goals for the future.

In fact, Angie is currently working on realizing those goals. Dexter, the Healthy Choice Deli employee who everyone knows and loves, is helping Angie to do the necessary paperwork to make her dream come true.

“Summer camp was my refuge as a child and I always admired my camp counselors. They made such an impression on my life and I wanted to hopefully do the same for some other children.”

Angie has many interests besides just camp counseling. “I love to do some shopping,” she says. “I’m dangerous.” “My favorite store to shop at is Rock Creek Outfitters.”

Angie also loves to travel. “That’s probably my second love, summer camp being the first,” she says. “Last summer I went to New Orleans, Niagara Falls, New York city, Washington D.C., and Boston. I’ve also been to Canada and Italy and I’m going to Jamaica this summer.”
Angie also plans on going to England to visit one of her close friends in the next two years. Angie was recently inducted as a Phi into Phi Mu fraternity. Angie says she first decided to rush because “all the girls seemed really sweet.”

On the weekends, Angie says she likes to “hang out with my girls in the eta class” and go home to spend time with her friends.

“I also like to do loads and loads of volunteer work. If I could live on that that’s probably what I’d do,” she says. Also on the weekends, Angie loves to “make crafts and be domestic.”

90210 is Angie’s favorite television show. Steve and Donna are her two favorite characters. Angie says that her “all time favorite movie” is The Never Ending Story. She also likes Purple Rain, Return to Paradise and Losing Isiah.

She says her favorite actress is either Sandra Bullock or Julia Roberts. Sean Connery is her favorite actor. “He’s a sexy old man,” she says.
