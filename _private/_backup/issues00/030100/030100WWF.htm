WWF Returns to Columbus with electrifying show
James Hagler / Guest Sprots Writer

Finally the World Wrestling Federation made a trip to the Columbus Civic Center. In a show that featured big names such as The Rock, Kane, Triple H, X-Pac, Edge, D’Lo Brown with The Hoes, and Saturn along with several others, the WWF made its first trip to Columbus in years. Followed on Monday night by RAW is WAR in Atlanta, the superstars of the WWF put on a spectacular show for the sold out crowd at the Civic Center. Beginning with Gangrel and Median defeating the Mean Street Posse all the way to the main event which featured The Rock and Kane in a truly great win against the D-Generation X duo of Triple H and X-Pac, it was truly an electrifying show throughout, which ended with a fifteen minute speech by The People’s Champ, The Rock, in what he named The People’s City, Columbus.

On Monday night’s RAW is WAR, featured every week on USA at 9 P.M., the WWF put on another excellent show. This time all the superstars were there including the Tag Team Champions of the World the New Age Outlaws, European Champion, Kurt Angle, and Intercontinental Champion Jericoh accompanied by the Ninth Wonder of the World, Chyna. Matches of the night include the New Age Outlaws and Dudley Boyz against the high flying duos of the Hardy Boyz and Edge and Christian, Too Cool, consisting of Scotty Too Hotty and Grand Master Sexay, took on the Dean Malenko and Perry Saturn, Taz and Bossman ended up in a disqualification. The main event of the night was The Rock, Kane, and Cactus Jack defeating Triple H, X-Pac, and the Big Show. After all the tv cameras were off The Rock landed The People’s Elbow then pulled Kane back into the ring to follow it up with a People’s Elbow of his own.

All in all it was an exciting weekend of wrestling for Columbus and Atlanta.
