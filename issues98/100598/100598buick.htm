Golf anyone?
by Chris Anderson / Sports Editor

HOME

Hurricane Georges has finally passed and the weather should be clear for the Buick Challenge at Callaway Gardens this weekend.

When many people think of golf their attention ceases and they remember how fun it is to watch on T.V. Not! Well this is a great opportunity to see it in person and realize that the tube does not do the sport justice.

Callaway Gardens offers a beautiful backdrop for the tough Mountain View Course. Many of the professionals enjoy coming here just to relax and have some time with their families. However, when Thursday rolls around they know it is time to get serious.

The tournament has steadily grown over the years and now includes sponsorship from Buick and television coverage on ESPN. These additions have added to the increased field which includes Vijay Sign, winner of the U.S. Open, Davis Love, who won the tournament last year and Columbus native, Larry Mize.
