Campus Candid: Abel Walker
by Michael Cox / Editor-in-Chief

HOME

Abel Walker is one of the many talented students here at Columbus State University.

Abel is a new incoming freshman who came to CSU from LaGrange, Georgia. He is a graduate of the class of 1998 from Troup High School in LaGrange, GA and is currently an art major here at CSU.

Abel says that he chose Columbus State for its Art program.

Since Abel has only been here for a short while, he has only experienced a few things.

Abel mentions, "There are people from all walks of life here at CSU. This is much different than the groups you hang out with in high school."

Abel enjoys doing a lot of different things in his spare time. His hobbies include playing the guitar and being associated with music in general. He also enjoys drawing, naturally, since he has been doing it since he was a little kid and is also currently an art major.

Abel would like to do graphics for the music industry upon graduation from college.
