Golf team places in invitational

by Chris Anderson / Sports Editor
Mississippi State ran away with the 30th Annual G. Gunby Jordan Invitational this past weekend at Callaway Gardens. The event was a 54 hole tournament and included many leading Division I schools.

The Bulldogs took an early lead after two rounds and never looked back, finishing with an -8 (856).Leading the way was individual winner Alex Rocha who finished with a -5 (211). He won in a four hole playoff against Maryland golfer Keith Unikal.

Although Columbus State did not win, they finished a very respectable 3rd place.

The Cougars were the only Division II school competing in the tournament and were led by Jon Drewery who fired a third round 65.
