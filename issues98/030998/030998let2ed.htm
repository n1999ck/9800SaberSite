Letter to the Editor
Dear Editor,

I would like to inform the citizens of Columbus about the March of Dimes. A charity that helps prevent birth defects by raising funds for research and creating awareness.

There are numerous fundraisers taking place year-round. One of them is the “Walk For Babies” benefit, which will be held March 20, 1998. For more information about this event and others, contact the March of Dimes office. Every month is filled with fun ways to raise money to help prevent birth defects.

Little red buckets are placed around Columbus for donations to the March of Dimes. Please take this opportunity to give a donation and drop some change in the buckets. You may also notice advertisements about upcoming events.

There is always something taking place to help prevent birth defects at the March of Dimes. They need your help to aid them in their quest. If you are interested in being a part of this great cause, please contact Linda Zabo at 571-1111.

Sincerely.
John Evans
