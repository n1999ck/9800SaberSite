"What Dreams May Come"

by Brian Hale / Entertainment Editor
“Despite everything we have experienced and all we have seen, nothing can prepare us for the wonders of what lies beyond.” That is a quote from a website devoted to the movie “What Dreams May Come.”

Academy Award Winner Robin Williams’ next movie is going to take you on an amazing journey...through heaven and hell to rediscover the meaning of life and to find out what exactly love is.

Williams is joined by Oscar-winner Cuba Gooding, Jr., Annabella Sciorra, and Max Von Sydow. The movie is directed by Vincent Ward, and the screenplay done by Ron Bass. “What Dreams May Come” is based upon the book by Richard Matheson.

“What Dreams May Come” is a very different type of love story. It starts out with Williams’ character, Chris, dying. Once he has realized and accepted the fact that he has died, he must deal with the issue of being separated from his wife, Annie. If the movie follows the same path as the book, then this is going to be an incredible movie.

The budget for the project reached nearly $100 million. The official website has opened and the images are amazing. When you run through, scroll your mouse over each picture entirely because things are hidden everywhere. If you want to check out the site, it is http://www.whatdreamsmay.com/.

Look for the movie to be released before or on Christmas.
