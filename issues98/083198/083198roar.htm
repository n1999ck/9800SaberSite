I feel as if some action should be taken in regards to whoever is partaking in these destructive acts. However, whoever is responsible for these actions need to take into consideration the importance of the things that they own and how they would feel if someone destroyed anything of theirs. No one knows who is the responsible party, Greek or Non-Greek, but organizations actually take pride in what they do if they need to be reminded.
