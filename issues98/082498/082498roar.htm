I recently had a disturbing experience with the Financial Aid Office here at CSU. It all started when I tried to print an invoice simply for my class schedule. The new computer invoice we have told me that I was not registered. When I approached the administration office, I was instructed to report to financial aid. I waited in line at the financial aid office for over an hour, only to find that they had one person assisting students who acted as though every question was a burden. I was told that my federal financial aid paperwork, which was filed in March, had been misplaced and that I would have to file. Furthermore, I would have to pay for my classes and would be reimbursed after about a twelve week period. I do not understand why my paperwork was not filed properly or why I could not be assisted further in understanding why I would not be receiving the Hope Scholarship when I have a 3.8 GPA.

I believe it is time for our Financial Aid Office to realize that students are the reason they have a job and that they should treat us with respect.

CSU Student
