Golfers are victorious

by Chris Anderson / Sports Editor
Maybe it is a little presumptuous, but considering their performance throughout the year and this past weekend at Bull Creek, the CSU golf team is eyeing a seventh Division II National Championship.

Out of 12 tournaments throughout 97 and 98, the team has finished first place 4 times, in the top 10, eleven times, and only once in the top twenty at 17th. This is impressive considering some of the tournaments included Division I schools.

All of this culminated this past weekend at Bull Creek where the Cougars shot a 14 under par 562, edging 2nd place finisher USC Aiken by nineteen strokes.

Leading the way for CSU was the overall winner, Jaco Rall who finished at 9 under par and finishing a close second was William Hamilton at 3 under par.

The next tournament is the South Atlantic Regional, May 4 - 6 at Auburn Links.
