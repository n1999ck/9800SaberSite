CSU hosts Wellness Fair

by Bridgett Brumbaugh
Area businesses will be participating in The Wellness Fair coming to CSU beginning February 9.

The fair will begin with a Health Profile in Davidson room 222.

The Health Profile consists of blood drawing and testing. The blood will be tested for several things including cholesterol, uric acid, blood count, and triglycerides. The cost of the testing is $20.00 and an appointment is necessary.

The following week the main activities begin. On February 18 form 10:00 am to 2:00 pm. twenty four off campus organizations and 7 on campus organizations will be available to talk with students about their services in the upper floor of Davidson.

Anna Bellemare, owner of Anna’s Therapeutic Massage, will be participating in the fair.

Bellemare says, “It is important to make people aware that massage is not just a luxury, but a part of better health.” She also believes the fair is a good opportunity to educate people and allow people to see massage first hand. Last year Anna’s offered massages to students, Bellemare says she hopes to do the same this year.

Also available will be stress tests, anxiety tests, diabetes tests, a heart risk assessment, and a body fat consultation. There will exhibits, literature and films at the fair.

This event is free to students and there will be a tee shirt raffle.

For more information or to make an appointment for the health profile call Student Health Services at 568-2039.

Organizations participating in the Fair include: American Heart Association, Anna’s Therapeutic Body Massage, AIDS Awareness, Columbus Parks and Recreation, Pastoral Institute, Jay Brodwyn Chiropractor, Columbus Health Department, American Lung Association, Behavioral Medicine at Doctor’s Hospital, Audio Matrix, Hollis Care Center, Battered Women’s Shelter, American Cancer Society, St. Francis Hospital, RSM Project, Cory Everson’s Aerobics and Fitness, Sunday’s, Gold’s Gym, Al Anon, Red Cross, Doctor’s Hospital, Contact, Georgia Bariatrics, The Tower Center, BSN Department of Nursing, Counseling Center, Career Center, Dental Hygiene, Greeks, and Rape Crisis.
