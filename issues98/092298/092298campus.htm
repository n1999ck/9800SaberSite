The Columbus State University campus has come a long way since it’s beginning in 1958. Pictured is a 1963 photo of campus including the Gym(far left), Woodall Hall(top), Howard Hall(middle right), Tucker Hall(bottom right), and Plant Operations(bottom center). The picture on the right is the most recent photo of campus and depicts just how much our campus has grown.

In addition to the several advances CSU has made over the past 40 years, new projects are in the works. The new RiverCenter for the Performing Arts has just begun construction downtown. The groundbreaking for the new Physical Education Complex will be held in October. Also, paper work has just started for the new Commerce & Technology building to be built adjacent to Davidson Student Center.
