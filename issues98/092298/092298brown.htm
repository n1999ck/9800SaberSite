Dr. Frank Brown interviewed

by Angel Jackson / Staff Writer
Dr. Brown has been President at Columbus State University for over ten years. He grew up in Alabama and attended both Florida State University and the University of Alabama.

Dr. Brown first came to CSU as Vice President of Business and Finance. He was appointed President six years later after President Brooke resigned.

Exciting is the word that Dr. Brown uses to describe Columbus State University. “So much is going on. We’re offering degree programs that have never been offered before. We’re reaching out to the community and working hard to better serve the students.”

The 40th Anniversary of CSU couldn’t have come at a better time according to Dr. Brown. “The school is expanding. There’s going to be a lot of construction going on during the upcoming years. It’s good that we can take time to celebrate and make plans for the future.”

With strong academic programs and good staff support, Dr. Brown hopes that students feel the pride of being at Columbus State University.
