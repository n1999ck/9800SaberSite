Cross Country victory

by Chris Anderson / Sports Editor
Columbus State won their first cross country meet last weekend at the Georgia Military College Invitational in Milledgeville,GA.

The men and women placed all runners in the top ten of the competition. Leading the way for the men were Geoffrey Rotich,1st (17:55), Abraham Bitok, 2nd (18:49), and Titus Rugut, 3rd (19:15). Other runners included: Mark Caldwell, 6th (23:38) and Bob Robinson 9th (25:20).

The women were led by Danerys Encarnacion, 2nd (19:43),Megan Grinder, 3rd (20:10),and Cheryl Mitchell, 4th (20:14). Rounding out the top ten were Sachiko Patrick, 7th (20:35), Francis Prieto, 9th (20:51), and Liz McKnight, 10th (21:00).

Both teams look to continue to improve as they travel to Atlanta for the Georgia State Invitational.
