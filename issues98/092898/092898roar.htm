I have a question for all of you that eat in the cafeteria on a regular basis- have you ever eaten in the Oxford Room? For those of you who don’t know where to find this room, it’s of to the side of the main dining area; a dimly lit room, with books along the walls to give it that scholarly, intellectual feel. Now that you know what I’m referring to, I’d like to ask the question again- have you ever eaten there? Well, if you are not a music major then I doubt not. It seems that each year (or at least the years I’ve been here) that the Oxford Room has been designated for this small, select few. There is no sign on the door stating that it is off limits to “other people”, but take one step through the door and, if you are not a part of their “group”, then you will not feel welcome. As the old saying goes, “Actions speak louder than words” and the actions of these students are as cold to outsiders as a piece of ice to the skin. It is said that music speaks in all languages- perhaps one day these people will learn to speak the language of kindness and offer a friendly hello to all those that enter their domain.
