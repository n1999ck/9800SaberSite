Educator of the Year announced
Press Release / Office of Public Relations, CSU

Columbus State University has named Shawn Cruzen, assistant professor of astronomy, as Educator of the Year. The announcement highlighted the April 30 Scholastic Honors Convocation in Fine Arts Hall. Other top honorees included Bill Chappell (Faculty Service Award), Mary Lindquist (Faculty Research and Scholarship Award), student Anna Mion (Faculty Cup) and Emily Nerness (Phi Kappa Phi and Academic Recognition Awards). CSU students react to Colorado tragedy
Clarissa Andrews / Staff Writer

Just before noon on Tuesday, April 20, two students from the “Trenchcoat Mafia” at Columbine High School in Littleton, Colorado, began an attack that left fifteen dead, including themselves. By now, everyone has seen the reports of the sawed-off shotguns, pipe bombs, and the many other weapons used in this killing spree. Across the country, students and parents are terrified at the idea of the same thing happening in their towns. Everyone is asking, “What could have caused those young men to kill thirteen innocent people?” The students at CSU are no exception. MORE
