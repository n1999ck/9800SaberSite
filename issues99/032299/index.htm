Foundation encourages kidney function testing
Press Release from the National Kidney Foundation of Georgia

Atlanta, GA — Kidney disease is an epidemic in Georgia, where 8,000 individuals must undergo kidney dialysis three times each week, and each dialysis treatment session typically lasts five hours. Over 650 Georgia residents are awaiting kidney transplants, yet only one in three will receive the kidney they so desperately need. MORE
