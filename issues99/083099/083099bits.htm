Music Bits: Former Grankie goes to Hollywood
Clarissa Andrews / Staff Writer

Frontman Holly Johnson is making waves in the music world again, and has a new album being released in the U.K. MTV reported that the album will be called “Soulstream,” and the first single will be the track titled “Disco Heaven.” The album is his first in nearly a decade, and the single is due out on September 6. 

In the early 90’s Johnson discovered he was HIV positive and dropped his musical projects to spend more time painting and working on his autobiography. 

Sheryl Crow has always shown that she has good musical taste, and now she has proven it again. Crow had a jam session with The Artist (formerly known as Prince) at the Lilith Fair stop in Toronto last week. The former Prince joined Crow on guitar and vocals during the performance of her hit song “Everyday Is A Winding Road.” 

This is not the first time Crow has been joined on stage by some of music’s greatest. In the past few months, Crow has shared the spotlight with Bob Dylan, Eric Clapton, Willie Nelson, and Chrissie Hynde, the Pretenders’ frontwoman. 

Speaking of The Artist, his former label Warner Brothers will once again try to cash in on his old name and material by releasing “The Vault... Old Friends 4 Sale.” 
“Vault” is a collection of ten previously unreleased tracks, a few of them dating back to 1985.

In other music news, it appears that I have done something terrible, evil, wrong, or just been a jerk to one too many people. God is punishing me for something. How do I know this? Well, Melanie C., better known as Sporty Spice of the painfully annoying group called the Spice Girls, has a solo album coming out. MTV reported that Sporty will be making several club appearances in New York to support her new album. I had to have done something really bad to deserve this. I am sorry.
