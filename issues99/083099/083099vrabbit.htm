CSU student theater group to tour
Lauren Jones / Staff Writer

A cast of five CSU theater students making up the Children’s Theater Youth Tour will be performing the fulfilling childhood classic, “The Velveteen Rabbit.” In addition to offering the production to the general public, the group will be touring Georgia and Alabama schools during fall semester. 

The cast consists of Melissa Walker as the Velveteen Rabbit, Wes Stanfield as the Boy, Joanna Poole as the Nanny, Gerald Gibson as the Train, and Jen Todd Camp in the role of the Mouse. 
This group of energetic actors will portray the tale of a toy rabbit’s journey under the direction of Brenda May. The story book pages are transformed to life with the craftsmanship of set designer Lee Maples, costume designer Steve Graver, and manager Molly Henderson.

CSU’s Children’s Theater Youth Tour starts off Thursday, September 9 for a local day care. The show will open for the public on Friday, September 10 at 9:30 a.m., Saturday, September 11 at 2:00 p.m. and 8:00 p.m., and Sunday, September 12 at 2:00 in the Davidson auditorium. CSU students and staff are admitted free with student ID, adult $5, and children $3. For a group rate or any other questions call the box office at 569-3179. 

Throughout the rest of the semester the cast will be touring Tuesdays and Thursdays sharing their talents with students across the state.

Don’t miss this heart-warming tale of love, innocence, and life.
