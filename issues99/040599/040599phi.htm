The top ten reasons why I love being a part of Phi Mu Kappa Sorority
Brandy N. Williams / Phi Mu Kappa

1. I always have a sister who can be called upon for guidance with anything. (school, work, parents, friends, relationships, etc ...)

2. The sorority has instilled qualities in me that will make me better qualified for jobs as a college graduate. (leadership, communication, and organizational skills)

3. The sorority’s need for its members to achieve academic excellence has made my G.P.A. a constant issue for me.

4. I have learned the true meaning of “Friends Forever and Sisters for A Lifetime”. Most women have experienced a “back-stabbing~ friend at some time in their life. It is nice to know that I finally have a group of girls that will never treat each other in a disrespectful way.

5. Phi Mu Kappa is a true-to-heart group of girls who would do anything for each other and that is why they are unique.

6. My sorority is a fun-loving group. They know how to have a good time, which is important. Socials are a blast if Phi Mu Kappas are any part of them. Anything from the Crush Party, Founders Day Ball, The Rose Carnation Formal, Karaoke Night, Pajama Party, no matter what it is, it will be fun if my sisters are there!

7. Rush is one of my favorite times of the semester; it is when potential pledges get a chance to see why Phi Mu Kappa is different from everybody else. I love to watch a girl fall in love with the sorority for what it is growing to be and for what it stands for.

8. I enjoy fundraisers as well (yes, I said fundraisers!). If my sisters are involved, then it will be enjoyable. Fundraisers are also a great way for me and my sisters to develop and use our enterprising skills.

9. Philanthropy is one of my favorite things to do with my sisters. It is a great bonding experience to get out there and work in the community. Charity and hard work are a few more lessons that most college students are not taught in the classroom. These lessons are priceIess and shouldn’t be traded for all the credit hours in the world!

10. Lastly the most important reason why I love being part of Phi Mu Kappa sorority at Columbus State University, or maybe I should say the 31 most important reasons are as follows: Cori, Heather, Ana, Lei, Tracie, Lynn, Terri, Jenny, Kelly, Jaime, Stacey, Katie, Rachel, Karen, Eli, Josie, Molly, Dawn, Carla, Jacquelynne, April, Jessica, Adrienne, Laura, Robin, Stephanie (Epsilon), Tabitha (Mascot), Kim (Honorary in my eyes!), Jenny (Holy One), Kelly (Princess), and Stephanie (Roomie)!!! T.M.T.Y. I LOVE YA’LL!!!
