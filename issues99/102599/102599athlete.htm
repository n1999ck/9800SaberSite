1999: Played in 46 games while starting 32. She had five sacrifice bunts. She scored a career high three times vs. Fort Valley State while collecting career best in hits (2) and runs batted in (3) against West Georgia.

High School: A local product from Shaw HS where she lettered in softball for Coach Debbie Ball for three years and in basketball for two. Her Raider softball squads placed fifth in the state in 1997 followed by a fourth place finish in 1998. Laura was a second team All-State selection as a senior and was a member of the south squad in the Georgia High School All-Star contest as well as a Who’s Who in Sports selection. She also captured All-Bi-City honors as a senior.

Personal: “Gard” is the daughter of Francis and Ann Gardner. She has one brother and two sisters. She plans to major in criminal justice at CSU. In her spare time, she likes to run, swim and workout.
