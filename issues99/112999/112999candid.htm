Freshman Jim “Jimaliscious” Hughes is this week’s Campus Candid. Jim is a music major and percussionist here at CSU. Jim’s other musical abilities include playing the piano, which he has done for thirteen years, and playing the guitar, which he has done for six years. 

Jim has been playing the drums for five years and says he started because he is “going to be a rock star.” After his career as a rock star is over, Jim’s goal is to “be a perverse old man and pick on young women.”

Jim is from Mcdounough, Georgia where he lives with his parents, their four dogs Wink, Katie, Susie, and Peanut, and their two cats Obe-Wan Kenobe and Leerie. Jim says he decided to come to CSU because “I got an apartment and I hate dorms.”

Music is Jim’s main interest. His favorite type of music is rock and roll, and his favorite bands are U2 and Led Zepplin. His all-time favorite CD is U2: The Best of 1980-1990.

Jim is the drummer for a band called “Five O’clock Shadow” that he and his friends started about three weeks ago. They used to call themselves “Fallout Shelter,” but then they discovered that the name had already been taken. Jim says that he and his band “don’t have a practice room anymore because the music professors kicked us out.”

Jim also adds that he “marched drum and bugle corps with the Spirit of Atlanta,” where they “took fourteenth place at the 1998 world championship.”

When Jim is not playing the drums, he says he enjoys skiing and partying. He also “played soccer for three years, basketball for five years, football for one year, and baseball for nine years.

On the weekends Jim says he likes to “watch the grass grow and clip my toenails.” When asked how he likes living in Columbus, Jim says to refer to his answer about what he does on the weekends.
Jim has traveled to France twice and Spain once. He is also rather proud to have been in Gatwick Airport in London once. 

Jim also wanted to “give a shout-out to my boys at C-12” and “BC and the other Waffle House waitresses.”
